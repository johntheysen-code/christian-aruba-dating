# Amor y Fe — 90-Day Launch Playbook

A concrete, week-by-week plan to take Amor y Fe from "live but empty" to "200 active members." Built for a solo founder on Aruba.

---

## Guiding principle

> **Trust before scale.**

On a small island, one trusted recommendation is worth a thousand impressions. The pastors, ministry leaders, and respected singles you can win in the first 90 days will carry the next 12 months of growth.

We will resist the urge to "launch loudly." We will launch quietly, deeply, with the right people first.

---

## 📊 The 90-day goal

- **30 days:** 30 vetted members (invite-only beta)
- **60 days:** 100 members, 3 endorsing pastors
- **90 days:** 200 members, 5 pastors, 1 press feature

These numbers feel small. They are intentional. Trying to get 10,000 members in 90 days from Aruba alone is a fantasy; trying to get 200 *real* members who shape the culture of the platform is achievable and worth more.

---

# 🗓 MONTH 1 — Foundation & invite-only beta

**Goal: 30 hand-picked members. No public marketing yet.**

### Week 1 — Personal beta list

- [ ] Sit down and list 30 Christian singles you trust personally — friends, family, friends of friends, church connections.
- [ ] Send each of them a personal WhatsApp / DM with one paragraph:
  > "I've been quietly building something for Christian singles on Aruba — would mean a lot to get your honest opinion. Takes 10 minutes. Promise it's not a hookup app. Link: [site]. Let me know what you think?"
- [ ] Track in a simple spreadsheet: name, sent, signed up, took quiz, feedback.

### Week 2 — First feedback round

- [ ] Personally message every signup. Ask 3 things:
  1. What confused you?
  2. What would make you delete the app?
  3. Who else should we invite?
- [ ] Take notes. Don't get defensive. Iterate on the site within 48 hours.

### Week 3 — Pastor list

- [ ] Make a list of 10 pastors / singles ministry leaders on Aruba you have any kind of warm intro to (or can get one to).
- [ ] Print 15 copies of **pastor-pitch.md**.
- [ ] Send 3 meeting requests this week.

### Week 4 — Iterate + first pastor meeting

- [ ] Have your first pastor meeting. Whatever they say, send a thank-you within 24 hours.
- [ ] Apply the most-requested fix from beta members.
- [ ] Tally: how many of your 30 are *active* (took quiz + visited Discover at least twice)?

**Month 1 success looks like:**
- 30 signups
- 20 active (logged in twice or more)
- 2 pastor meetings held
- 1 product improvement shipped from feedback

---

# 🗓 MONTH 2 — Trusted endorsement & soft public launch

**Goal: 100 members, 3 endorsing pastors.**

### Week 5 — More pastor visits

- [ ] 5 more pastor meetings this week. Send pitch ahead of time.
- [ ] Ask each pastor: "Would you mention this once in your singles ministry?"

### Week 6 — Pastor newsletter / bulletin

- [ ] Of the pastors who said yes, follow up: give them ready-made copy for their bulletin or WhatsApp.
- [ ] Sample bulletin blurb is in `copy-samples.md`.

### Week 7 — Soft public launch

- [ ] Post on **your personal LinkedIn** with the story: *"Why I'm building a Christian dating app in Aruba."* (Draft in `copy-samples.md`.)
- [ ] Post in 2-3 relevant Aruba Christian Facebook groups (with admin permission).
- [ ] Update the live site if needed for the moment.

### Week 8 — First "second wave"

- [ ] Ask your active members to invite 1 friend.
- [ ] Add a simple "invite a friend" message in the Welcome email when it's built.
- [ ] Hold an informal Zoom with 5-10 most engaged members. Ask what's missing.

**Month 2 success looks like:**
- 100 total members
- 50+ have taken the compatibility quiz
- 3 pastors actively recommending
- First mutual matches happening
- 10+ unsolicited "thanks for building this" messages

---

# 🗓 MONTH 3 — Press & community growth

**Goal: 200 members, 5 pastors, 1 press feature.**

### Week 9 — Press pitch

- [ ] Pitch **Diario** (https://www.diario.aw/) — email the lifestyle / community reporter.
- [ ] Pitch **Bon Dia Aruba** (https://bondia.com/) — same approach.
- [ ] Pitch a local radio show that covers community stories.
- [ ] Pitch template in `copy-samples.md`.

### Week 10 — Member spotlight content

- [ ] Get permission from 2-3 willing early members to share their story on Instagram (anonymously is fine if they prefer).
- [ ] Post one a week for the next 4 weeks.

### Week 11 — Local event

- [ ] Find a local Christian event (youth retreat, singles night, church gala) and ask if you can have a small table.
- [ ] Bring printed pitch deck + a QR code for the site.

### Week 12 — Retro & 6-month plan

- [ ] Look at the data: who's matching, who's messaging, who's gone silent.
- [ ] Survey active members: what features do they wish existed?
- [ ] Plan the next 90 days. Maybe it's: more locations, in-person meetups, premium features. Decide based on data.

**Month 3 success looks like:**
- 200 total members
- 5+ pastors endorsing
- 1 press feature
- 30+ matches
- 10+ active conversations
- A clear list of what to build next

## 📋 What to track every week (a 10-minute exercise)

Open a simple Google Sheet with these columns. Fill it in every Sunday night.

| Week | New signups | Active members | Quiz completions | Matches | Messages sent | Pastor meetings | Press inquiries | Top complaint |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

That's it. No fancy dashboards. The signal is in the trends.

---

## 🎯 The single most important habit

> **Talk to one new user every week. Voice call or in person. Not chat.**

You will learn more from one 20-minute conversation with a real user than from 1,000 page views in your analytics dashboard. Don't skip this. It's the unfair advantage of being a small, local founder.

---

## ⚠️ What to avoid in the first 90 days

- ❌ Paying for ads (you don't have signal yet; you'll just waste money)
- ❌ Trying to "go viral"
- ❌ Building new features users haven't asked for
- ❌ Comparing yourself to Hinge or Tinder's numbers
- ❌ Quitting when growth feels slow

The first 30 days WILL feel slow. That's normal. Trust the process.

---

## After day 90

You'll have signals on what's working. Common paths from here:

- If pastor channel works → double down, scale to 10 more pastors
- If press works → invest in PR + thought leadership
- If member referrals work → build a real referral system
- If retention is great → invest in feature improvements
- If retention is poor → pause growth, fix retention first

The data will tell you. Listen to it.
