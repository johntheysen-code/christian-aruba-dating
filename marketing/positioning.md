# Positioning — How Amor y Fe is Different

What we stand for, who we're up against, and why we win for the specific Christian single on Aruba.

---

## Positioning statement

> For Christian singles on Aruba who are serious about marriage,
> Amor y Fe is a faith-first dating community
> that matches you on the convictions and values that actually predict a lasting marriage —
> unlike swipe-first apps that reward shallow attraction

## Why Amor y Fe wins for our audience

### 1. We're built for marriage, not browsing.

Christian Mingle has a quiz, but it's mostly demographic. Amor y Fe's compatibility quiz covers **what actually matters in a Christian marriage**: Scripture authority, prayer life, marriage timing, children, carnival, Sabbath, intimacy, cohabitation, media, tattoos, and more. The match score is the result.

### 2. We're restricted by design.

Matching is **one man, one woman.** This isn't a feature — it's a foundational decision that aligns with the biblical view of marriage and signals seriousness to our audience. Members never have to wonder if the app is for them.

### 3. We're local.

The most underrated advantage. On a 120,000-person island, a dating app that recognizes Oranjestad vs. San Nicolas, that references the divi-divi tree, that speaks Papiamento — that's a different category from a global app pretending to be local.

### 4. We're modest.

Our **gradual-reveal** model hides extra photos, full bio, and faith details until both parties have mutually liked each other. This protects users' privacy and aligns with Christian courting values — you don't share your deepest convictions with anyone who happens to swipe by.

### 5. We're real people.

Facebook sign-in is the only way in. Every member is a verified real person. No anonymous accounts, no catfishing.

## Objection handling

**"I'm not sure I want a dating app."**
→ It's not Tinder. There's no swiping. Take the quiz, see if anyone is compatible, decide from there. You can delete your account in 10 seconds.

**"Is this for serious dating or hooking up?"**
→ Marriage. Every part of the product, from the quiz questions to the messaging restrictions, is designed for people who want to find a spouse.

**"Won't I just see the same people I already know on Aruba?"**
→ Probably some — and that's good. Many people are surprised by who shares their faith convictions when they finally read someone's profile. The quiz reveals depth that small talk never does.

**"Is my information safe?"**
→ Profile is only visible to other signed-in members. Messages are private to matched users. Block and report tools are one tap. Self-serve account deletion erases everything permanently.

**"What if I'm divorced / widowed / older?"**
→ You're welcome here. Faith convictions matter at every life stage, and our matching respects the journey you've been on.
