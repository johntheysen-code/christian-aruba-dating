# Amor y Fe — Brand Brief

The single source of truth for what we are, who we serve, and how we sound.

---

## Name

**Amor y Fe** *(Spanish/Papiamento friendly — meaning "Love and Faith")*

Pronounced *ah-MOR ee FAY*.

## Mission

To help Christ-centered singles on Aruba find a spouse who shares their faith, their values, and their way of life — without the swipe-and-pray culture of secular dating apps.

## Vision

Every Christian single on Aruba who wants marriage has a clear, intentional, faith-aligned path to find their equally yoked partner.

## Tagline

**Equally Yoked, by design.**

Secondary line (Papiamento):
*Traha pa nos dushi Aruba.* — "Made for our sweet Aruba."

## Promise

> Less swiping. More marriage.

---

## Target audience

**Primary:** Christian single adults living on Aruba who are actively seeking marriage — at **any life stage**, from their 20s through their 70s and beyond.

**Sub-segments we serve well:**

- Young professionals just back from studies abroad
- Singles in their 30s who feel local dating options are exhausted
- Divorced or widowed Christians of any age looking for a second chance with shared faith
- **Believers 50+ entering a new chapter** — never written out of the marriage story
- Newcomers to the island who need to find community

Faith and seriousness about marriage are the qualifiers. Age is not.

**We are NOT for:**

- Casual hookup seekers
- Long-distance global daters
- Non-Christians (welcome to sign up, but the matching values won't fit)

---

## What makes us different

1. **Marriage-first, not swipe-first.** Compatibility quiz, gradual reveal, profiles built for depth.
2. **Restricted to one man + one woman.** Aligned with a biblical view of marriage.
3. **Convictions matter.** The quiz covers carnival, Sabbath, cohabitation, media — not just denomination.
4. **Local to Aruba.** Built for the island's specific church community, in our languages.
5. **Mascot you remember.** The shoco couple — Aruba's national bird, faithful pair-bonders.

## Voice & tone

| When we write… | We sound… | We don't sound… |
|---|---|---|
| Homepage headlines | Confident, warm, biblically literate | Salesy, corporate, generic Christian |
| Onboarding emails | Like a thoughtful pastor friend | Like a marketing funnel |
| Social posts | Hopeful, intimate, slightly playful | Preachy, judgmental, hype |
| Pastor outreach | Respectful, practical, brief | Pitchy, salesy |
| Member messages | Encouraging, never desperate | Pushy, FOMO-driven |

**Voice tells:**

- We use the word "believer" more than "Christian" (warmer)
- We say "marriage" out loud — never "relationship goals"
- We reference Scripture sparingly and accurately (no random verse drops)
- Papiamento accents are welcome ("dushi", "topa", "pareha") — but never gimmicky
- We never mock other dating apps; we just stand apart

- Logo (couple, snuggling, heart above)
- Hero illustration (separated owls, beach)
- Empty states (single waiting owl)
- Match celebration (reunion owls with hearts)
- Quiz intro (couple studying together)

## Do's and don'ts

### Do

- Lead with the differentiation: depth, convictions, marriage
- Reference Aruba specifically (towns, churches, beaches by name)
- Use Papiamento and Spanish naturally
- Celebrate marriage as the goal
- Be specific (a real verse, a real church, a real founder story)

### Don't

- Use "soulmate" or "the one" language (sounds saccharine)
- Reference other dating apps by name in marketing
- Use stock photos of generic couples (especially non-Aruban)
- Quote Scripture out of context for sales pressure
- Promise "find your spouse in 30 days" or anything similar

## Quick reference — one-liner answers

- **What is Amor y Fe?** A Christian dating app built for singles in Aruba.
- **How does it work?** Take a 22-question compatibility quiz, then meet believers who actually share your faith, values, and way of life.
- **How is it different?** No swiping. No casual encounters. Restricted to one man and one woman. Quiz covers convictions, not just denomination.
- **Who is it for?** Christian singles on Aruba seeking marriage.
- **Cost?** Free, with premium features possibly added later.
