# Pastor Pitch — One-Page Leave-Behind

Print this. Hand it to a pastor. Walk out. (Then follow up in a week.)

---

## How to use this document

1. **Print on one sheet, double-sided is fine.** Carry 10 copies.
2. **Make it personal:** before any meeting, write the pastor's first name at the top in pen ("For Pastor Carlos —").
3. **The meeting goal isn't to "close" them.** It's to (a) show respect, (b) explain what we are and aren't, and (c) ask one specific question (see end).
4. **15 minutes max.** Pastors are busy. Be early. Bring nothing except this paper and a phone with the live site ready.

# AMOR Y FE
## *Christian dating, designed for Aruba.*

---

### Why I built this

Hello Pastor [Name],

I'm a Christian, a member of [your church or denomination if relevant], and like many singles on this island, I've watched friends — and myself — struggle to find a faithful partner here. Tinder, Bumble, and even Christian Mingle weren't built for us. They reward swiping and surface chemistry, not the convictions that hold a Christian marriage together.

So I built **Amor y Fe** — *Love and Faith* — a dating site designed from the ground up for Christian singles on Aruba who want marriage, not casual encounters.

I'd like a few minutes of your time to show it to you, and ask if you'd consider letting your singles ministry know it exists.

---

### What Amor y Fe IS

- A **free** dating site for Christian adults on Aruba
- Members sign in with **Facebook** — every profile is a verified real person
- Each member takes a **22-question compatibility quiz** covering Scripture, prayer life, church attendance, carnival, Sabbath, intimacy, marriage timing, children, and lifestyle
- The site **shows compatibility scores** so members can focus on people who actually share their values
- Matching is **restricted to one man and one woman**, in keeping with a biblical view of marriage
- Messaging is **only available after a mutual match** — no cold outreach

### What Amor y Fe is NOT

- ✗ Not a hookup app
- ✗ Not swipe-based
- ✗ Not for casual dating
- ✗ Not open to anonymous accounts
- ✗ Not affiliated with any denomination (welcoming to all Christian traditions)

### How we protect your congregants

- Personal details (full bio, additional photos, faith details) are **hidden until two members mutually like each other**
- One-tap **block and report**
- **Self-serve account deletion** at any time
- Photo verification through Facebook
- Privacy policy and terms of service on the site

## ❓ Anticipated questions (for the spoken meeting, not the page)

**"Why not just leave dating to the church community?"**
The church does its part. But many singles — especially those in their 30s, divorced, or new to the island — have exhausted who they know. Amor y Fe complements the work the church already does; it doesn't replace it.

**"What if my congregants get hurt by the experience?"**
Every product carries risk. We've built in privacy by default (gradual reveal), block/report tools, and instant deletion. We're also actively looking for feedback from pastors on how to make members safer.

**"Will you ever charge for it?"**
Core matching, messaging, and the quiz will always stay free for our community. We may add optional premium features later — but never a paywall on finding a spouse.

**"How do you verify a member's faith?"**
We can't verify belief. What we can do is design every part of the product so casual or non-Christian users self-select out: the quiz is faith-explicit, the matching restricts to opposite-gender, the language is biblical. We trust the design to surface serious believers.

**"What if I want to be involved more deeply?"**
I'd love that. We're looking for a small group of "pastor advisors" who'd give feedback on new features and help shape community standards. No financial ask, no formal commitment.

- **Schedule a 1-week follow-up** to check in. Pastors get busy. A gentle nudge is fine; pushiness isn't.
