dating app out there, hoping one of them would surface a believer who shared
not just my faith, but the way I live it out.

filtered on "religion" with a single checkbox. None of them asked: do you
keep the Sabbath? do you tithe? do you want to be married within two years?

or mutual partnership?

So I built one that does.

Amor y Fe — Love and Faith — is a dating app designed from the ground up
for Christian singles on Aruba who want marriage, not casual encounters.
Take a 22-question compatibility quiz. See your match score with every
other member. Talk only after a mutual match. It's free.

Built with two iconic Aruban touches: a divi-divi tree and a shoco couple
(faithful pair-bonders).

Link in comments. I'd love your feedback — especially the hard kind.

🌳 #ArubaTech #ChristianDating

## How to use these

1. **Tweak before you publish.** Generic copy is the enemy. Add a real name, a real church, a real verse.
2. **Test 2-3 variants** of headlines. See which one gets responses.
3. **Read out loud** before sending. If it sounds like a Tinder ad, rewrite.
4. **Bring your voice.** These are starting points. The best version is the one that sounds like you.
